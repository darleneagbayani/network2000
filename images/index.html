About

Since 1998, Network 2000, LLC. has been a technology solution company focused on meeting the needs of customers throughout the state of Hawaii. Locally owned, we are committed to providing quality service and to build long term trusted relationships with our customers. We are blessed with a wide range of resources , one of the largest teams of certified engineers in the state, trusted and loyal partners, physical offices on every major island, and a wealth of experience that spans 4 decades.
