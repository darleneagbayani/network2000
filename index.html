Who is Network 2000?

Network 2000 is a full solution technology company. Our establishment is locally owned, and has the largest team of certified technical professionals in Hawaii. We pride ourselves on delivering expert IT services as well as a great customer experience. With offices located on each major island, our team is ready to handle your IT needs.

Since 1998, Network 2000, LLC. has been focused on meeting the needs of customers through Hawaii. We are committed to providing quality services and building long-term and trusted relationships with our customers.

Commited to providing quality service and building trusted relationships.
